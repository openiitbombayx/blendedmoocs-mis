Welcome to IITBombayX

Registering with IITBombayX gives you access to all of our current and future free courses. Not ready to take a course just yet? Registering puts you on our mailing list - we will update you as courses are added.

Next Steps

As part of joining IITBombayX, you will receive an activation email. You must click on the activation link to complete the process. Don't see the email? Check your spam folder and mark IITBombayX emails as 'not spam'. At IITBombayX, we communicate mostly through email.

Need Help?

Need help in registering with IITBombayX? View our FAQs for answers to commonly asked questions. Once registered, most questions can be answered in the course specific discussion forums or through the FAQs.
